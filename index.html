About Me

Sazzadul Islam is currently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University. He has an exceptional academic background and numerous achievements in his field. His passion for Electrical and Electronic Engineering led him to pursue a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), where he achieved an impressive CGPA of 3.9515/4.00, reflecting his dedication to academic excellence.

Sazzadul Islam's research experience is extensive, with a notable achievement being his undergraduate thesis on Quantum Transport and Device Physics, completed under the guidance of Dr. Mahbub Alam. Currently, he is pursuing his M.Sc. and conducting his thesis under the supervision of Dr. Ehsanur Rahman, focusing on biosensor technologies based on Tunnel Field-Effect Transistors (TFETs) and High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMTs).

In addition to his academic accomplishments, Sazzad has received several prestigious awards and honors. He consistently earned the Dean's List Award at BUET for his outstanding academic performance and has been the recipient of multiple scholarships, including the Talentpool Scholarship at both the secondary and higher secondary levels. Sazzad was also the winner of the BUET EEE Olympiad in 2023.

Sazzadul Islam's achievements extend beyond academics, as he has actively engaged in extracurricular activities and held various leadership roles. He served as the Membership Development Coordinator of the BUET Robotics Society from 2022 to 2023, and subsequently took on the role of Deputy Head of Event Management from 2023 to 2024. During this time, he played a key role in organizing and managing events for the society.
